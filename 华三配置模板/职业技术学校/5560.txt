interface range GigabitEthernet 1/0/21 to GigabitEthernet 1/0/26

interface range GigabitEthernet 1/0/28 to GigabitEthernet 1/0/28
port link-type trunk 
port trunk permit vlan all
port trunk pvid vlan 10
quit
